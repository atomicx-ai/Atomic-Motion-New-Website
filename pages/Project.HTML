Candidate Blood
Services

Canadian Blood Services plays a vital role in the Canadian healthcare system. By collecting and testing blood, plasma, stem cells, and organs, CBS provides Canadians with the lifesaving treatments they need to survive and thrive.


Atomic Motion was entrusted with the mission of transforming Canadian Blood Services’ digital presence from the ground up. This comprehensive project included a full redesign of their website’s look, functionality, and user experience. The objective was to create a modern, intuitive platform that would not only reflect the organization’s critical mission but also significantly boost public engagement, awareness, and participation in their wide range of programs and services. Through innovative design and strategic digital enhancements, Atomic Motion helped position Canadian Blood Services for greater impact and accessibility across the country.

       Client:  Canadian Blood Services – a national organization dedicated to providing life-saving biological products for all Canadians in need.
       Objective:  To transform and modernize their online presence through a complete website and app redesign.
       Deliverables:  A fully reimagined, user-friendly digital platform that significantly boosted awareness and participation in CBS’ programs.
